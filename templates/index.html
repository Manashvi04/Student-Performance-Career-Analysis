About The Institution

Charotar University of Science & Technology

Established under the Gujarat Private University Act of 2009, CHARUSAT is a premier education institution built on a 125-year legacy of social service by the Shri Charotar Moti Sattavis Patidar Kelavani Mandal.

Recognized as a "Centre of Excellence" by the Government of Gujarat and proudly holding a prestigious "A+" Grade accreditation from NAAC, we are deeply committed to blending the scholarly serenity of ancient learning centers like Takshashila with the robust rigor of modern educational powerhouses.

Our Vision

To become a dynamic global institution in a knowledge-driven world through excellence in teaching, research, and social contribution—transforming Charotar into a Global Education Hub.

Our Mission

To foster an intellectual culture that develops graduates prepared for a life of purpose, service, and leadership. We aim to cultivate "Job Creators and not Job Seekers" armed with honesty, integrity, and accountability.

The Intelligent Performance Tracker

In pursuit of fostering global excellence, CHARUSAT has integrated an advanced predictive academic software system tailored specifically for our B.Tech students. This platform centralizes 4-year academic tracking and bridges the gap between active coursework and future career opportunities.

Holistic Tracking

Both academic CGPA and non-curricular activities are comprehensively tracked over exactly 8 designated semesters.

Career Alignment

The system intelligently maps your performance patterns against historical trends to anticipate and align your strengths with ideal future roles.

Faculty Oversight

Professors have complete access to dynamic departmental analytics, allowing targeted intervention and support precisely when needed.
