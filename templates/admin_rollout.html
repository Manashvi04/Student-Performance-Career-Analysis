⚙️ Semester Rollout Engine

Automated batch processing for academic promotion & retention based on CGPA constraints.

Execute Academic Rollout

The Rollout Engine sweeps through the entire student database, identifying the highest logged active semester for each student. It evaluates their Semester GPA (SGPA). If the SGPA mathematically clears the minimum passing threshold (≥ 5.0), the student is officially promoted and their current_semester state is incremented. Otherwise, they are marked as FAILED and retain their current status to repeat the semester block.
